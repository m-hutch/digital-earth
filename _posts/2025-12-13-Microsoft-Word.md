# Microsoft Word
Microsoft Word is available through the Microsoft Office 365 software package. UT Dallas students can install this software for free using their NetID login.

## Using Microsoft Word online
You can also use Microsoft Word online in your browser. This video walks you through setting up a personal account for free: [How to Use Microsoft Word for FREE Online](https://youtu.be/X7ur7a14FQg){:target="_blank"}

Remember, UTD students do not need to make a new account. You can sign in with your email (NetID@utdallas.edu) and NetID password.
