# Transfering files from a phone to a computer
1. Download the **One Drive** app from the App Store or Google Play Store.
2. Open the One Drive app and sign in with your UTD account (NetID@utdallas.edu).
3. Upload the photos or files your want to transfer to One Drive using the app.
4. On your computer, open a browser and navigate to [https://onedrive.live.com/login](https://onedrive.live.com/login){:target="_blank"}.
5. Sign in to One Drive using the same account you signed in with on your phone
6. Download the files you want to your computer. 


# Transfering files from one computer to another
1. On the computer with the files you want to transfer, open a browser and navigate to [https://onedrive.live.com/login](https://onedrive.live.com/login){:target="_blank"}.
2. Sign in with your UTD account (NetID@utdallas.edu).
3. Upload the files you want to transfer.
4. On the second computer where you want the files, open a browser and navigate to [https://onedrive.live.com/login](https://onedrive.live.com/login){:target="_blank"}.
5. Sign in with the same account you used on the first computer.
6. Download the files you want onto the second computer.
