# Check your work

1. Review the checklist on your lab report to make sure you have completed each item and have placed your initials next to each completed item. *If there are items you were unable to complete, do not place your initials next to that item*

2. Review the assignment rubric on eLearning to make sure you understand how the assignment will be graded

# Upload & submit

1. Upload all required documents in the same submission. **Only the last submission will be graded**:
    - Lab01_Report.docx

    <span class='alert'>Your lab report must be submitted as a `.docx` file. DO NOT submit a `.pages` file or a `.pdf` file
    </span>

2. Submit the assignment

3. Check your email to make sure you received the submission confirmation receipt. Do not delete your confirmation receipt.
